# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Fitness enthusiasts and gym-goers in India who want to make informed purchasing decisions about protein supplements. They visit the site while actively shopping — often on mobile — to compare live prices, verify lab test results, and find the best cost-per-gram value within their budget. Primary context: quick decision-making during a purchase journey, not casual browsing.

## Product Purpose

ProteinAggregator is a live comparison engine for Indian protein supplements. It scrapes real-time pricing and stock data from major brands, ranks products honestly by cost-per-gram (both claimed and lab-verified), and surfaces lab test reports so users can verify brand claims. The goal is to cut through marketing hype and help buyers find genuine value.

## Brand Personality

Minimal, modern, transparent.

- **Minimal:** Every element earns its place. No decorative noise, no redundant information, no clutter.
- **Modern:** Clean typography, confident spacing, contemporary dark interface that feels like a premium tool rather than a bargain site.
- **Transparent:** Data is presented honestly — lab results, price history, stock status. No manipulation, no hidden agendas.

## Anti-references

- **E-commerce clutter:** No Amazon-style dense grids, excessive "BUY NOW" buttons, popups, fake urgency timers, or "Only 2 left!" badges. This is a comparison tool, not a store.
- **Generic SaaS admin panels:** No sidebar navigation, no data tables with 20 columns, no B2B dashboard feel. Keep it consumer-focused and approachable.
- **Bro-science / aggressive gym aesthetic:** No red/black aggressive vibes, no "GET SHREDDED" typography, no overly masculine or macho imagery. The tone is smart and informed, not aggressive.

## Design Principles

1. **Data over decoration:** The interface exists to present comparison data clearly. Every visual choice should make the numbers more readable, not hide them behind effects.
2. **Trust through transparency:** Lab verification badges, clear source attribution, and honest stock status build user trust. Never obscure or sugarcoat data.
3. **Speed of comprehension:** Users should understand which product is the best value within 3 seconds of landing. Hierarchy, contrast, and grouping serve this goal.
4. **Mobile-first pragmatism:** Most users browse on phones. The layout must work flawlessly on small screens before being enhanced for desktop.
5. **Restrained confidence:** A premium feel without pretension. Dark theme, solid colors, clean typography — no gradients, no glassmorphism, no decorative animation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance: minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratios for body text, 3:1 for large text and UI components
- Keyboard navigation support for all interactive elements
- Screen reader friendly: proper heading hierarchy, ARIA labels on data-heavy cards, alt text on badges
- Reduced motion preference respected: no layout animations for users who prefer reduced motion
- Mobile-first responsive design: touch targets minimum 44x44px, readable at 320px viewport width
